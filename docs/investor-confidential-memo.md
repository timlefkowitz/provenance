## 1. Company overview

Provenance is building the infrastructure for artwork provenance and professional data (artists, collectors, galleries). We enable verification, CV and grant management, and lead capture so the art world can operate on trusted, structured data.

## 2. Market

Art market participants (artists, collectors, galleries, institutions) lack a standard way to prove and share provenance and credentials. We serve that need with a SaaS platform and future API.
